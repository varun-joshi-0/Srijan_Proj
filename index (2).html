Our Story

Fighting Hunger,
Restoring Dignity.
One Meal at a Time.

Apna Roti Bank Charitable Trust is a volunteer-driven movement ensuring no one sleeps hungry. Every day our volunteers prepare and distribute freshly cooked meals across our communities.

Beyond food, we restore hope and belonging — proving that the smallest act of kindness creates a lasting impact.
