Our Story

A movement built on compassion

Apna Roti Bank Charitable Trust works to fight hunger and support underprivileged communities through food distribution drives, volunteer initiatives, and community engagement.

Every meal shared creates dignity, hope and a stronger society. We believe no act of giving is too small — a single roti can change someone's day.

Regular food distribution drives across communities
Volunteer-led grassroots outreach programs
Family support during emergencies and festivals
