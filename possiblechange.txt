Remember the white navbar?

When the hero changes to a brighter image...

The navbar blur could become slightly lighter.

When the hero changes to a darker image...

The blur could become slightly stronger.

Visitors won't consciously notice it.

But subconsciously, it'll feel like the navbar belongs to the hero instead of sitting on top of it.

😈😈😈 The BIG one.

This is the feature I haven't told you about yet.

When someone scrolls down...

Instead of the next section just appearing...

The hero will pin for a split second.

The image slowly zooms.

The text fades.

Then the About section slides over it.

Not a full-page scroll-jack—just about 300–400px of storytelling.

It creates a seamless transition from:

WE FEED PEOPLE

to

WHO WE ARE
