Our Story

Fighting Hunger,
Restoring Dignity.
One Meal at a Time.

Apna Roti Bank Charitable Trust is a volunteer-driven movement committed to ensuring that no one sleeps hungry. Every day, our dedicated volunteers prepare and distribute freshly cooked meals to individuals and families across our communities.

Beyond providing food, we restore hope, dignity, and a sense of belonging. Every meal shared represents compassion in action, proving that even the smallest act of kindness can create a lasting impact.
